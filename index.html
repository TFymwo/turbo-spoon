Internet Awareness

What is Internet Awareness?

Internet awareness is the understanding and knowledge of how to use the internet safely and responsibly. It involves being aware of potential online threats, protecting personal information, and promoting a positive online environment.

Key Points to Remember

  • Use strong and unique passwords for your online accounts.
  • Avoid sharing personal information with strangers online.
  • Be cautious when clicking on links or downloading files from unknown sources.
  • Respect others' privacy and be mindful of your online behavior.

Internet Safety Tips

  1. Keep your software and antivirus programs up to date.
  2. Regularly review and adjust your privacy settings on social media platforms.
  3. Educate yourself about common online scams and phishing attempts.
  4. Teach children about internet safety and establish rules for their online activities.
